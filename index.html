Welcome to Citizen and Constitution!

Idea Description
Video explanations Quiz assesment Decision Making

The "Nagrik Aur Samvidhan" platform aims to simplify key aspects of the Indian Constitution through engaging, gamified formats. It will be accessible, user-friendly, and inclusive, featuring multimedia and language translation. Deliverables include a functional prototype, user feedback, and a detailed development report. The platform seeks to raise constitutional awareness among citizens of all educational levels.

Overview of our project

Process

1

Introduction

- User Onboarding: Start with a welcome screen explaining the learning objectives related to the constitution and citizenship.

- Select Topic: Users select the topic they want to learn about (e.g., rights, duties, fundamental principles, etc.).

2

Video Learning Module

- Watch Introductory Video*: Provide a short video that explains the selected topic in a simplified manner.

- Progress Indicator*: Show users their progress (e.g., 1/3 videos completed).

- Engage with Explanation*: After each video, provide an option for users to read a detailed text explanation of the videoâ€™s content to reinforce learning.

3

Quiz for Assessment

- Interactive Quiz: After each video or a set of videos, provide quizzes with multiple-choice questions, true/false questions, or short-answer questions.

- Instant Feedback*: After answering, users receive instant feedback (e.g., correct, incorrect, explanation).

- Track Performance*: Let users know their quiz scores and track performance over time.

4

Additional Resources

-Extended Explanation: After the quiz, provide further explanations, related articles, or links to legal texts from the constitution for users who want to dive deeper into the topic.

- Discussion or Forum: Optionally, provide a space for users to ask questions or engage in discussions with other learners.

5

Review and Certification

-Progress Review*: Users can review their quiz results and watch videos again if necessary.

-Completion Certificate*: Upon finishing a module, provide a digital certificate of completion or a summary of their learning achievements.

6

Advanced Learning Path

-Unlock Next Topic: Once one section is completed, the next module/topic is unlocked.

-Structured Learning Path: Offer a structured learning path from basic to advanced knowledge of the constitution and citizenship.
